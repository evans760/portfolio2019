Online Form Submission

ADURO programs have a variety of forms that must be accurately captured in order for the participant to correctly proceed through their Well-Being program. How might we allow participants and/or their healthcare provider to easily & securely submit forms associated with their programs in a way that ADURO can verify the authenticity of the data while providing the user with the feedback necessary for the participant to be certain the data was captured by ADURO’s systems.
